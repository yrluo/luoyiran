Yiran Luo (罗怡然), Ph.D.

I am currently a Postdoctoral Associate at the MMSS Research Team, the Department of Geomatics Engineering, UofC, Calgary, Canada, where I received the Ph.D. degree in 2020. I also obtained the Ph.D. degree with the School of Information and Electronics, BIT, Beijing, China, in the same year. After that, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow at the IPNL of the Department of Aeronautical and Aviation Engineering, PolyU, Hong Kong SAR, China.

Research Interests:
- SR GNSS signal processing & remote sensing
- Advanced GNSS SDR design
- Urban/indoor positioning & navigation
